Drying Up a Storymap About Droughts in California

Drying Up was created using ArcGIS Storymaps with the bivariate map being made in QGIS. Drying up tells the story of the growing drought crisis happening across the US but specifically in Southern California. I wanted to really highlight inequalities that are happening across our entire culture down to the things that we use on a daily basis and while one group may be forced to change another group is allowed to continue. Through this piece I want to help shape how people can perceive complex problems as maps can be a great way to bridge the gap to public understanding and with more knowledge and easier to understand knowledge comes better decisions making.
