The Battles of Alfred

The Battles of Alfred is a historical map created as a final project for an introductory cartography class. This map is based upon the lectures of an English history class that I took as a sophomore. The map itself was made in ArcMap and heavily stylized in illustrator. The icons are of my own design and were created in illustrator as well. Alongside lecture material other sources that were used included the Anglo-Saxon Chronicle which is a narrative written from the English side about history of the time. I really enjoy this map as it combines my interest in history with making maps. It also shows how accessible maps make different disciplines. This map may not be too enjoyable if it were written as just a bulleted list but the map itself adds a visual element that I feel mapmaking is all about when representing data.
