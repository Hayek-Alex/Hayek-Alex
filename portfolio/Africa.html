A Physical Map of Africa

The Physical Map of Africa was made in ArcMap and finalized in Adobe Illustrator. This map was created as a project in an “Introduction to Cartography” class. The piece was used to showcase labeling and label placement and show the use of Illustrator as a useful tool in placement and design aesthetics.
