About Me

Hi! Thanks for checking out my portfolio. My name is Alex and I am currenly a senior studying Cartography, GIS, and Geography at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My favorite part about being a Cartographer is being able to tell a narrative about complex issues and data by bringing together multiple disciplines to help people understand a multitude of different things. I think that Cartography presents a unique opportunity to showcase knowledge and make it a fun and engaging experience for viewers.

Outside of making maps, I enjoy traveling the worlds. It's my goal to travel to every continent and (hopefully!) every country in the world. I want to be able to try the foods of each unique culture and region as I think that food tells a lot about personal experiences and helps us to learn about eachother. Besides travelling, I'm an avid fisherman and can spend countless hours out on a lake or on the ocean. I'm also very involved in the hockey community and hope to coach youth hockey when I graduate!
